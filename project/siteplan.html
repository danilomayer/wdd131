Site Plan

Site Name: Danilo Mayer - Personal Portfolio

It's a simple way to recognize the purpose of my project.

Site Purpose

The Personal Portfolio Website will showcase my skills, projects, and experiences. It will be designed to appeal to potential employers, clients, or anyone interested in my work. The website will be dynamic, responsive, and adhere to web development standards, providing a comprehensive demonstration of my mastery in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Scenarios

What can he do? The site will provide specific information on my skills both on programming and other experiences I have had.
How can I contact him? The portfolio will also provide direct links to all my social media, Github profile, linkedIn, Whatsapp.
